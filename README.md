This project, titled "Digital Literacy Portfolio," is designed to demonstrate my proficiency and responsibility as a Student Digital Ambassador at VIT Bhopal University. The primary objective is to showcase a comprehensive understanding of digital tools, professional online presence, and cybersecurity awareness. The scenario involves acting as a peer-mentor who can navigate modern digital platforms while advocating for safe and ethical internet practices.
Throughout the five modules, I have executed tasks ranging from creating visual awareness infographics to setting up professional technical portfolios on GitHub and LinkedIn. Furthermore, the project addresses critical real-world challenges, such as UPI payment fraud and professional email etiquette, ensuring that I am equipped to handle the digital demands of an Integrated M.Tech in Cybersecurity. This portfolio serves as a verified record of my ability to collaborate, communicate, and protect data in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Module Summary
Module 1: Digital Communication & Design Creation of an original, high-impact infographic using Canva. This task focused on summarizing complex digital literacy concepts-such as Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and Phishing awareness—into a visually accessible format for a student audience.
Module 2: Professional Digital Presence Establishment of a professional identity across industry-standard platforms. This involved optimizing LinkedIn for networking and configuring a GitHub Profile README to showcase technical interests in Cybersecurity and UAV/EW systems.
Module 3: Technical Platforms & Collaboration Demonstration of coding proficiency and data collection. Tasks included solving algorithmic challenges on HackerRank to verify programming logic and utilizing Google Workspace (Forms & Sheets) to create collaborative data-driven tools.
Module 4: Professional Etiquette & Networking Mastery of formal digital communication. This module involved drafting high-stakes professional emails (internship inquiries and extension requests) and creating a Social Media Safety Checklist to manage digital footprints and privacy.
Module 5: Cybersecurity Awareness & Ethics Analysis of real-world threats and mitigation. This included a detailed case study on UPI/Payment Fraud, identifying the psychological triggers of social engineering, and compiling an 8-point prevention guide linked to the National Cyber Crime Portal (1930).
